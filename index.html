About

Classic match-3, tuned for adventure.

At its core, Skull Treasures Match 3 Puzzle is simple: match pieces, clear objectives, and move forward. I’m a sucker for the whole treasure hunting vibe — something about those skull symbols just works for me. The graphics are bright and colorful, and when you pull off a big combo, it really pops on screen. It’s built to be approachable, but it rewards players who plan their moves and learn how to set up bigger chains.

Features

Everything you want in a match-3 — plus a little extra.

  • Match-3 with unique twists

    The basic gameplay’s still there, but they throw in new stuff that makes you think differently about each level. Some stages ask for careful clearing, others reward aggressive combo-building. You won’t get bored doing the same thing over and over.

  • Rewards that feel earned

    When you beat a level, it actually feels like you accomplished something instead of just going through the motions. Rewards support your next attempt, helping you build confidence as challenges rise. The game keeps you moving without making every win feel identical.

  • A world built around treasure

    The theme isn’t just decoration — it shapes the mood. It's got this cool adventure feel where each level is like uncovering another piece of the puzzle. It’s light adventure energy with a puzzle core.

Levels

Over 1000 levels, and they don’t all play the same.

Skull Treasures Match 3 Puzzle offers 1000+ levels designed to keep pushing your strategy. The first levels are pretty easy to get you used to things, but later on they really make you think about your moves. The best part is that the challenge doesn’t come only from difficulty — it comes from variety. You’ll keep encountering new tasks that force you to rethink your usual approach.

  • Clear specific tiles and blockers

  • Reach targets with limited moves

  • Build combos to trigger chain reactions

  • Use boosters tactically instead of instantly
